About RickXpress

RickXpress is a Ratnagiri’s-first ride booking platform that offers support in small towns like Khed, Chiplun, Dapoli, Mandangad, Guhagar and even nearby villages — making daily travel easier for everyone.

We provide fast and affordable transportation: Auto Rickshaw, Cab and Bike — along with long-distance routes connecting to major Indian cities.

Why Choose RickXpress?

  • Designed specially for Konkan region & smaller towns
  • Book your preferred driver manually
  • Transparent fare before booking
  • Multi-language: English, Hindi, Marathi, Urdu
  • Verified drivers and vehicles
  • Works even in low network areas common in villages
